I am the second-year Master student in Computer Science at Stanford University, where I work under the supervision of Prof Dorsa Sadigh at ILIAD Lab.

This summer, I worked as a Research Intern at Nvidia Applied Deep Learning Research Team and studied how to extend EfficientZero to continuous locomotion tasks with Raptor, a large distributed RL framework, while using less samples and less computation.

In 2020, I received my B.S. in Information Security from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. After graduation, I took a gap year and worked as RA at Shanghai Qi Zhi Institute, advised by Professor Yi Wu. In 2019, I studied as an exchange student at UC Berkeley and worked as a summer intern at BAIR Lab, advised by Professor Pieter Abbeel.

Please feel free to check out my resume and contact me with email if you want!
